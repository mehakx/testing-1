Page 1 — Order / Grid as Stage

A strict grid turns collage into a “stage.” Elements align, scale, and tilt with intent. Hover and tab through images to reveal captions. Links and embedded media hint at hypertext roots.

Theme: City signals → inner signals — trains, notes, stickers, and a holographic “hair” motif that flows across pages.

Why grid?

Structure lets contrast pop. When everything snaps to a system, the smallest misalignment becomes meaning.
